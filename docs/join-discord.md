Our Discord server is the best place to discuss our  API, get help from community devs,
find collaborators, and promote your projects using the API!

## How to Join our Community on Discord

1. Go to <a href="https://discord.gg/startgg" target="_blank">discord.gg/startgg</a> to join our server
2. Go to <a href="https://discord.com/channels/339548254704369677/342709909881749505" target="_blank">#welcome</a> and react with the 3rd Party Developer role to post in the developer channels
3. Ask questions in <a href="https://discord.com/channels/339548254704369677/541015301618401301" target="_blank">#api-questions</a>, post your project in <a href="https://discord.com/channels/339548254704369677/517423958283517954" target="_blank">#dev-projects</a>, or discuss other developer topics in <a href="https://discord.com/channels/339548254704369677/545822746307985429" target="_blank">#dev-general-chat</a>
